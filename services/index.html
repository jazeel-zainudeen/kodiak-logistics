Freight Forwarding

Air, Ocean, and Rail Freight: Tailored solutions for time-sensitive or high-volume shipments.

Cross-Border Expertise: Customs clearance, documentation, and regulatory compliance for GCC, China, and Europe.

Customs Brokerage & Compliance

Hassle-free customs clearance in GCC countries, including UAE's advanced paperless systems (e.g., Dubai Trade Portal).

Expertise in China's export regulations and Europe's Union Customs Code (UCC).

Warehousing & Distribution

Smart warehousing in Dubai’s strategic free zones (JAFZA, DAFZA) for GCC distribution.

Temperature-controlled storage, bonded warehouses, and just-in-time inventory management.

Last-Mile Delivery

GCC-wide road logistics with real-time tracking for retail, e-commerce, and industrial sectors.

Project Logistics Services

Customized Project Strategy: Tailored logistics solutions covering every stage—from pre-shipment planning to final installation.

Customs, Permits, and Documentation: Expert management of complex customs procedures and regulatory requirements.

Supply Chain Consulting

Route optimization, cost reduction strategies, and risk management for China-Europe-GCC trade flows.
